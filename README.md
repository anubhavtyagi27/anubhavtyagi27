Product leader with **10+ years** of experience building **fintech and enterprise SaaS platforms** across payments, lending, and asset management. My work has focused on complex workflow-driven products spanning API platforms, financial data systems, and AI-enabled automation used by operational teams.

I have led distributed teams and built products from early-stage development to enterprise deployments across banks, fintech companies, and asset managers.
